When visiting the beach, Felicity observed that a breeze blew in from the ocean during the day and out toward the ocean at night. Which factor is most responsible for the direction of land and sea breezes in coastal regions? (A) the revolution of Earth around the Sun (B) the uneven heating between the land and water (C) the gravitational pull of Earth on the atmosphere (D) the daily rise and fall of tides in a regular pattern
Answer: B
