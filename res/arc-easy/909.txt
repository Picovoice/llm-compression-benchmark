Marta and Roger are comparing how long it takes two different types of seeds to sprout. They plant both types of seeds in similar pots, place them in a sunny window, and water them daily. If they want to conduct their investigation correctly, which information is most important to record? (A) the depth of the soil (B) the temperature of the water (C) the number of days until the seeds sprout (D) the size of pot used for growing the seeds
Answer: C
