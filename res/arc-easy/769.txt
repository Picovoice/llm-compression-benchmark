A student wrote the following claim on a homework assignment: "Eukaryotic cells need more lipids than prokaryotic cells." Which statement below provides the BEST support for her claim? (A) Eukaryotic cells have a nucleus. (B) Eukaryotic cells make up multicellular organisms. (C) Eukaryotic cells contain membrane-bound organelles. (D) Eukaryotic cells perform cellular respiration.
Answer: C
