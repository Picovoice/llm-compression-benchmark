Indigo buntings are birds that nest in Florida during the summer. Male buntings are bright blue in color while females are a dull brown. How does the dull brown color of the female buntings help the species survive? (A) It helps male birds find a mate. (B) It helps the birds find food more easily. (C) It helps hide the birds while they sit on their nests. (D) It helps the birds find the flock when migrating.
Answer: C
