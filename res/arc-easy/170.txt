During a walk in the woods, Mandy finds a plant structure that had fallen onto the trail. Mandy can tell the object contained seeds, and thinks it came from a flowering plant instead of a conifer. How can she determine if the object came from a flowering plant? (A) by looking for seed coats on the seeds (B) by looking for wing-like structures on the seeds (C) by looking for fruit tissue around the seeds (D) by looking for young sporophytes inside the seeds
Answer: C
