A glass of ice water is placed on a table. After 10 minutes, there are drops of water on the outside surface of the glass. Which change in phase caused the drops of water? (A) Liquid water in the air evaporated into a gas. (B) Liquid water in the air condensed into a solid. (C) Water vapor from the air evaporated into a liquid. (D) Water vapor from the air condensed into a liquid.
Answer: D
