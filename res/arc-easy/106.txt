In their science classroom, Sam and Julia cross a heterozygous tall (Tt) pea plant with a homozygous short (tt) pea plant. What ratio represents the results Sam and Julia can expect? (A) 1 tall plant to 3 short plants (B) 2 tall plants to 2 short plants (C) 3 tall plants to 1 short plant (D) 4 tall plants to 0 short plants
Answer: B
