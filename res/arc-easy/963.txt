Two students went fishing one morning and caught 20 fish. The afternoon before, they caught 5 fish. Because of their morning success, they claimed that morning was the best time to fish. Which of these would most likely disprove their claim? (A) catching more than 20 fish in the same location (B) catching more than 20 fish in another location (C) catching more than 20 fish the next afternoon (D) catching more than 20 fish the next morning
Answer: C
