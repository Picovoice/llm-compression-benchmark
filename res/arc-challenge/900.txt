After many decades of being absent from Yellowstone National Park, gray wolves are being reintroduced. As the wolf populations have increased, elk and bison populations are beginning to decrease. Which is another consequence of the reintroduction of wolves that is likely to occur in Yellowstone Park? (A) Both elk and bison will become extinct. (B) Populations of scavengers will decrease. (C) Bears and coyotes will become more numerous. (D) Populations of plants consumed by elk and bison will increase.
Answer: D
