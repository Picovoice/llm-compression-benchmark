A student is investigating how light behaves when it strikes different surfaces. She has a frosted window glass pane, a clear window glass pane, a mirror, and a piece of cardboard. Which of these objects will scatter the most light? (A) the mirror, because it is reflective (B) the piece of cardboard, because it is opaque (C) the clear window glass, because it is transparent (D) the frosted window glass, because it is translucent
Answer: D
