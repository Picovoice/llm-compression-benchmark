Antonia is testing the properties of block A. • She puts block A on top of a hot metal block and then touches the block A with her finger. • She keeps her finger on the block A for several minutes, but block A still does not get hot. What can Antonia conclude about block A? (A) It is a good conductor. (B) It is a poor conductor. (C) It is highly magnetic. (D) It is nonmagnetic.
Answer: B
