Which statement best describes the process of respiration? (A) Oxygen and sugar are used in the process that provides energy to cells; water and carbon dioxide are its waste products. (B) Water and sugar are used to in the process that provides energy to cells; oxygen and carbon dioxide are its waste products. (C) Oxygen and carbon dioxide are used in the process that provides energy to cells; sugar and water are its waste products. (D) Carbon dioxide and sugar are used in the process that provides energy to cells; water and oxygen are its waste products.
Answer: A
