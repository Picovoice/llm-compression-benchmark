Nonaggressive birds called purple martins nest each spring to lay eggs. Starlings are aggressive birds that will eat the purple martin's eggs and nestlings. Which will most likely be the effect on both populations after a starling invasion? (A) Purple martins will increase and starlings will decrease. (B) Purple martins will increase and starlings will increase. (C) Purple martins will decrease and starlings will decrease. (D) Purple martins will decrease and starlings will increase.
Answer: D
