The number of living organisms in and around a lake remained fairly stable throughout the month of June. In July there was a three-week period of high temperatures and no rainfall. This resulted in an overabundance of green algae that almost covered the surface of the lake. This would most likely result in (A) a decrease in the amount of bacteria. (B) a decrease in the amount of oxygen. (C) an increase in the water temperature. (D) an increase in the fish population.
Answer: B
