The "Ring of Fire" is a long chain of volcanoes that encircles the Pacific Ocean. Which of the following is the best explanation for the arrangement of these volcanoes? (A) They outline the crater caused by the impact of an asteroid. (B) They follow the shape of the adjacent tectonic plates under the sea. (C) One volcano usually triggers a second volcano with its hot magma. (D) Volcanoes always form in rings because of underwater convection currents.
Answer: B
