Nutrient-laden waters from heavy rains flow into the Gulf of Mexico. This produces an algae bloom in the ocean environment. As the algae decays, it uses up large amounts of oxygen along the coastline. Which will most likely happen to the area as a result? (A) Nitrogen will replace oxygen in the water. (B) The temperature of the water will increase. (C) Wave action will carry away the dead algae. (D) Bottom dwellers will die and fish will swim away.
Answer: D
