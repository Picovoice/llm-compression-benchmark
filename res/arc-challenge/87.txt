Which of the following statements is a scientific hypothesis? (A) Flossing has proven to be an effective method of improving oral health. (B) Many infectious diseases, such as gum disease, are caused by microorganisms. (C) In general, any method that reduces bacteria in the mouth will improve oral health. (D) An individual who flosses daily will be less likely to get gum disease than one who flosses weekly.
Answer: D
