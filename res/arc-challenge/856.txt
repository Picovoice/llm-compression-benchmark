Several different sized filter-paper disks are soaked in a solution and placed into another solution where a reaction will take place that produces a gas. The gas bubbles collect on the filter paper and make the filter paper rise to the top of the solution. Which of these is a measurement that would not be made during this experiment? (A) the number of bubbles produced (B) the distance the filter paper rises (C) the diameter of the filter-paper disks (D) the time it takes for the filter paper to rise
Answer: A
