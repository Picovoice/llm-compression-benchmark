In science class, a student obtains 20 mL of an acid solution. A small amount of acid is added to a substance to detect the presence of copper. What should be done with the excess acid solution? (A) Pour the acid into a trash can. (B) Neutralize the acid before pouring it down a sink. (C) Return the acid to the teacher's stock bottle. (D) Allow the water to evaporate from the acid solution.
Answer: B
