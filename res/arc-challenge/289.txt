A school replaced all of their light bulbs with a new type of light bulb. The new light bulbs use less energy. How will the new light bulbs most likely help the environment? (A) The amount of light pollution will decrease. (B) The air conditioner at the school will run less often. (C) Fewer natural resources will be used to make electricity. (D) Money will be saved on the electricity bill for the school.
Answer: C
