A pharmaceutical company developed a drug to help relieve headaches. Recently, the pharmaceutical company discovered that the drug may cause nausea in a small number of people who might take the drug. Which is the most ethical course of action that the drug company could take? (A) Discontinue the use of the drug. (B) Inform people of the possible side effects. (C) Reduce the amount of drug given to people. (D) Modify the drug to include medicine that prevents nausea.
Answer: B
