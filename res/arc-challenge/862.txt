Alicia put hydrogen peroxide and yeast in a paper cup. The paper cup felt hot, and she concluded that a reaction occurred that gave off heat. What else could Alicia do to get a more accurate conclusion? (A) use an insulated cup rather than a paper cup (B) change the amount of hydrogen peroxide used (C) take a picture of the cup after the reaction took place (D) use a thermometer to measure the change in temperature
Answer: D
