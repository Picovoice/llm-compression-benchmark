A student wants to show that the matter in a whole apple is equal to the matter in the pieces of the same apple. Which process best describes how the student can demonstrate the relationship between the matter in the whole apple and the apple pieces? (A) observing the apple pieces (B) cutting the apple into more pieces (C) observing the color of the whole apple, then observing the color of the apple pieces (D) determining the mass of the whole apple, then determining the mass of the apple pieces
Answer: D
