A chemical called DDT was once used to kill insect pests. When investigations showed this chemical was harmful to some types of birds, the use of DDT stopped. How was the scientific process best able to help scientists understand that DDT was harmful to birds? (A) Different predictions about the chemical were made. (B) Tests performed on the chemical had different results. (C) Repeated tests on the chemical reached the same conclusion. (D) Different procedures were used to test the properties of the chemical.
Answer: C
