A glass of water at 110°F is placed in a room next to a glass of water that is 50°F. The temperature of the room is 73°F at all times. If the two glasses were left in the room overnight, the temperature for both glasses of water the next day would be approximately (A) 43°F. (B) 50°F. (C) 73°F. (D) 90°F.
Answer: C
